Old English Rain

As it strung it's last orange and purple
A stray thought rode the calm string of the breeze
Under the glow of the evening dots and moon
The humming of the leaves as it traveled
in their slumber
As though an invite
But ears blocked, cause when will such
a chance grave itself
Slowly the nights cold shoulder filled with
bleeding warm lights
As humid stalks to cold and piercing
The thousands below slowly trickling to
hundreds, tens, soon though lifeless
Yet again found alone staring at the stained
But it's better than what it's detained to acknowledge as sanctuary
The string of music from the traveling breeze
Accompanied but what only can be shocks of the electric
To whispers in the deep below
Jamming together, both human and nature
mellowing to what it identifies as
A cry to slumber....

Architecture of Silence

Could say among all features on the mobile the notification pop up feature isn't the no. 1 pick
But on the other side of the coin
It's a major carrier
Whenit springs brief as it may, so does a name
And does it change the facial canvas
Some got your ear to ear, toes curling
Some got you like "... this why I was offline "
Some come bearing the promise of hours of talking
Others drifts you off reality, carrying deep nostalgia
Others, it's like sinking into bed after a long day
Astonishing, right?
A name, 2to 4 syllables carry such power
Drawing out all these emotions
Joy, spite, disgust, nostalgia
Yet I sway to the sinking onto bed nostalgic feeling
The comfort shared with the name's bearer
That smile knowing that "I can't wait"
Surfing ann urgency
An urgency to hit back
To start a banter, just for laughter
The reassuring statements
The company to give and receive
With no pressure no stress
Such that make you sigh, cause yes it's always that easy
The back to back,
Even when knowing what they gonna say but still you wanna hear
Cause it's another day, not yesterday nor last week
Another day to crave the simple stuff
The type where idiotic sharing IS communication
Where you could rant and they'd listen
A safe space where the conversation is soo seamless
Even when it goodbye, you hit up
" ..did I tell you.."
".. hope you know.."
For me it's a daily vitamin dose
Expect I don't need it for daily
I CHOOSE to need it for daily
The pop up feature carries the name, and every time it does with that annoying notification bell, I smile
Guess, it is my favorite feature
It's like the preview of worthwhile times
Of knowing "..here we go again"
Jumping head on cause damn is it worth it
It got you like"..gonna happen in .."
Of the two sides of the coin guess you know my my favorite

Fragmented Truths

There are rooms built entirely out of things left unsaid.
The pillars are dust motes dancing in pale morning illumination,
and the heavy ceiling is supported by a single collective breath.
Step softly across the uncarved flagstones,
for even a heartbeat here sounds like a hammer striking iron,
and the truth is only ever told in the spaces between lines.
We piece together realities from torn journals and loose margins.
A syllable found in the dark can rebuild an entire forgotten empire,
while grand declarations turn to ash in the sun.
Trust only the fragments that survive the fire,
the torn corners where signatures used to rest,
and the ink that bled deep enough to score the wood below.
