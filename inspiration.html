The Inspiration

The architecture of my vulnerability and the reason these words leave my desk.

Why I Write

The world dictates that we must remain strong, unyielding, and completely detached from our emotions—treating vulnerability as an outright flaw. So, where do I go to safely collapse into my own feelings?

I go to my books. I have filled over eleven journals completely with raw words that capture precisely how I felt in exact, fleeting moments of time.

These written archives allow me to relive chapters of my life just as vividly as the moments they were born, preserving the absolute pinnacle of my emotions.

My words refuse to let me forget. They constantly remind me that I am complex, no matter how simple or flat the outside world tries to define me. Facing different situations uncovers entirely different sides of who I am, serving as a dynamic record of my personal evolution and growth.

Why I Share

To those on the outside, I might look like an unshakeable stone wall. But behind that exterior sits a chaotic backend—thousands of intersecting thoughts running through my mind simultaneously that absolutely must find an escape route.

I choose to share these pieces of poetry and prose to dismantle that wall. By showing the raw backend of my internal world, I hope to prove to anyone reading along that they are allowed to let their own thousands of thoughts out, too.
