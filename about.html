Behind the Ink

Welcome. I am a collector of fleeting feelings and rainy thoughts. My writing balances on the fine line separating old English romanticism from modern vulnerabilities.

I gather inspiration from worn-out margins of antique novels, the rhythm of persistent rain against glass pane, and the raw texture of classical letters. This space holds everything my heart could not carry quietly.
